<p><p>Aluminum metal fencing is available in two main types: mesh and strip fencing. Mesh fencing consists of interwoven metal wires forming a sturdy barrier, typically used for security purposes or to enclose outdoor spaces like gardens or sports fields. Strip fencing, on the other hand, is made of narrow aluminum strips arranged horizontally or vertically, providing a sleek and modern look suitable for residential or commercial properties. Both types offer durability, low maintenance, and versatility in design options, making them popular choices in the fencing market.</p></p>
