<p><p>The Over The Air (OTA) Engine Control Module market offers various types of connectivity options for updating software wirelessly. This includes Embedded 3G or 4G Modem, Embedded Wi-Fi, and Smartphone OTA (Bluetooth or Wired Tethering). Embedded 3G or 4G Modem allows for updates to be sent directly through cellular networks. Embedded Wi-Fi enables updates to be downloaded over a local Wi-Fi connection. Smartphone OTA, using Bluetooth or Wired Tethering, allows updates to be transferred from a smartphone to the vehicle's engine control module.</p></p>
