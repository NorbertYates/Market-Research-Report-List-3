<p><strong>The Rotary Steerable Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Push the Bit</li><li>Point the Bit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rotary Steerable Systems market includes two types: Push the Bit and Point the Bit. In Push the Bit systems, the steering control is located near the bit, allowing for more precise and swift adjustments in the drilling process. On the other hand, Point the Bit systems have the steering control located at a distance from the bit, enabling better accuracy and stability while drilling. Both types offer advanced technology for efficient drilling operations, but differ in the placement of the steering control mechanism.</p></p>
