<p><strong>The Industrial Resin 3D Printer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LFS Technology</li><li>FDM Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Resin 3D Printer market is divided into two types: LFS Technology and FDM Technology. LFS Technology stands for Low Force Stereolithography, which utilizes a flexible film to enhance the printing process, resulting in high-quality prints with smooth surfaces. FDM Technology, on the other hand, stands for Fused Deposition Modeling, and involves melting a thermoplastic filament and depositing it layer by layer to create the desired object. Both technologies are widely used in industries for rapid prototyping, manufacturing complex parts, and producing functional prototypes.</p></p>
