<p><p>Nano UAVs, or unmanned aerial vehicles, belong to the emerging market of miniature drones. They are classified into three main types: fixed-wing, rotary-wing, and others. Fixed-wing nano UAVs resemble traditional airplanes, with wings and a streamlined body. They are highly efficient in terms of flight duration and can cover large areas. Rotary-wing nano UAVs, on the other hand, are similar to helicopters, allowing them to take off and land vertically, hover in place, and maneuver in tight spaces. Lastly, the "others" category includes unique designs that combine elements of fixed-wing and rotary-wing UAVs or incorporate unconventional technologies.</p></p>
