<p><p>Automotive UBI, or usage-based insurance, utilizes different types of data to determine insurance premiums. Odometer data-based UBI calculates premiums based on the actual mileage driven by the insured vehicle. GPS data-based UBI uses location and driving behavior data to evaluate risk. Other market types may include telematics devices that capture factors like speed, acceleration, and braking patterns. These various approaches help insurance companies offer more personalized and accurate pricing based on how an individual actually uses their vehicle.</p></p>
