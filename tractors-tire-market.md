<p><p>The tractors tire market offers three main types of tires - Pneumatic, Solid, and Polyurethane. Pneumatic tires are filled with air and provide a cushioned, smooth ride. Solid tires are made from solid rubber and are puncture-proof, but can be rough on uneven terrain. Polyurethane tires are lightweight and durable, offering a balance between the cushioning of pneumatic tires and the durability of solid tires. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the agricultural and industrial sectors.</p></p>
