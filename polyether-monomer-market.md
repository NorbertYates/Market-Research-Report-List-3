APEG (Allyl PEG Ether) is known for its superior reactivity and is used in the production of resins and adhesives. HPEG (Hydroxy Polyethylene Glycol) is a key ingredient in the manufacturing of water-based coatings and adhesives due to its excellent water solubility and low toxicity.</p></p>
