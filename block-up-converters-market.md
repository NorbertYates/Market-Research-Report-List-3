<p><p>Block Up Converters (BUCs) are electronic devices that are used to convert low-frequency signals to high-frequency signals in satellite communication systems. BUCs are essential components of satellite communication systems as they enhance the performance of satellite transponders.</p><p>The Block Up Converters Market is expected to experience significant growth during the forecast period.
