<p><p>The food and beverage industrial robotics market can be categorized into four types: articulated, Cartesian, SCARA, and Delta. Articulated robots are designed with multiple joints, allowing them to move and manipulate objects in a wide range of motions. Cartesian robots have three linear joints that move along perpendicular axes, enabling precise and controlled movements. SCARA robots are commonly used for assembly tasks with their horizontal arm movements. Delta robots are known for their high-speed and precision in pick-and-place operations. These different types of robots cater to various industrial needs and applications within the food and beverage sector.</p></p>
