<p><strong>The Anemometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Velocity Anemometer</li><li>Pressure Anemometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>An anemometer is a device used to measure wind speed. There are two main types of anemometers in the market: velocity anemometers and pressure anemometers. A velocity anemometer measures wind speed by calculating the change in velocity as air flows through the device. On the other hand, a pressure anemometer measures wind speed by determining the difference in pressure between the front and back of the device. Both types of anemometers are widely used in weather monitoring, research, and various industrial applications to accurately measure and analyze wind speeds.</p></p>
