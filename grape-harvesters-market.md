<p><p>There are different types of grape harvesters available in the market including the Pivotal Striker and Trunk Shaker. The Pivotal Striker is designed to gently remove grapes from the vines using rotating spindles, while the Trunk Shaker utilizes a mechanical arm to shake the trunk of the grapevine, causing the grapes to fall off. Both machines are used to increase efficiency and productivity during the grape harvest season, making it easier for farmers to gather their crop quickly and effectively.</p></p>
