<p><p>Hem flange bonding adhesives are available in two main types: one component and two component. One component adhesives are pre-catalyzed and ready to use, requiring no mixing before application. On the other hand, two component adhesives require mixing of two separate components before use. One component adhesives are convenient and easy to use, while two component adhesives offer more control over the curing process and are often used for more demanding applications. Both types have their own advantages and are chosen based on specific bonding requirements.</p></p>
