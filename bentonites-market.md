<p><p>Bentonite is classified into two main types based on its dominant cation: sodium bentonite and calcium bentonite. Sodium bentonite is known for its swelling and sealing properties, making it ideal for applications such as waterproofing, drilling mud, and foundry sand binders. On the other hand, calcium bentonite is more commonly used for its absorption and adsorption capabilities, finding applications in industries like animal feed additives, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals. Both types of bentonite play crucial roles in various industrial sectors due to their unique properties and characteristics.</p></p>
