<p><p>The Buzzer Driver Chip market consists of two main types: Electromagnetic and Piezoelectric. Electromagnetic buzzer drivers use an electromagnet to create sound when an electric current flows through it. They are commonly found in devices such as smartphones and alarm clocks. On the other hand, Piezoelectric buzzer drivers generate sound when an electric field is applied to a piezoelectric crystal. They are often used in applications requiring a high pitch and low power consumption, like medical devices and security systems.</p></p>
