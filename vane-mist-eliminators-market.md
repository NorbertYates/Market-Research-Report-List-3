<p><p>Vane mist eliminators are used for removing entrained liquid droplets from gas streams. They are categorized into two market types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical mist eliminators are installed in a vertical position and are typically used in applications such as boilers, evaporators, and chemical processing plants. Horizontal mist eliminators, on the other hand, are installed horizontally and are commonly used in natural gas separators, oil mist removal systems, and HVAC units. Both types serve the same purpose of efficiently separating liquid particles from gas streams, but they are designed and installed differently depending on the specific application requirements.</p></p>
