<p><p>The refueling nozzle market can be categorized into two main types: manual and automatic. In the manual refueling nozzle market, the nozzle operation is controlled by an operator who has to physically engage and disengage the nozzle with the vehicle's fuel filler. This type requires more effort and time from the operator. On the other hand, in the automatic refueling nozzle market, the nozzle operation is automated, with a mechanism that engages and disengages the nozzle without the need for manual intervention. This type offers quicker and more convenient refueling process for users.</p></p>
