<p><p>Gear motors are popular in various industries due to their ability to provide a high torque output. There are three main types of gear motors in the market. Parallel shaft gear motors have their input and output shafts parallel to each other, providing efficient power transfer. Right-angle shaft gear motors have their input and output shafts positioned at a 90-degree angle, allowing them to be used in tight spaces. Planetary gear motors are compact and deliver high torque with low backlash, making them ideal for precision applications.</p></p>
