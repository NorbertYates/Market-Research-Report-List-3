<p><p>Epoxy toughened adhesives are divided into two main types: rubber elastomer and thermoplastic resin. Rubber elastomer adhesives contain rubber particles that enhance flexibility and impact resistance, making them ideal for applications requiring vibration or stress absorption. On the other hand, thermoplastic resin adhesives offer high strength and durability, making them suitable for applications where maximum bond strength is essential. Both types of epoxy toughened adhesives provide unique properties to meet specific requirements in various industries.</p></p>
