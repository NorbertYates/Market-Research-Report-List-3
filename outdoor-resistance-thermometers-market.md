<p><p>Outdoor Resistance Thermometers are temperature measuring devices that are specifically designed for outdoor use. There are two main types of outdoor resistance thermometers - Threaded Resistance Thermometers and Flanged Resistance Thermometers. Threaded Resistance Thermometers have a threaded connection at one end, allowing them to be easily installed into pipelines or equipment. Flanged Resistance Thermometers have a flange connection at one end, enabling them to be mounted directly onto a pipe or vessel. Both types are highly durable and accurate, making them ideal for monitoring temperatures in outdoor environments.</p></p>
