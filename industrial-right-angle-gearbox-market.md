<p><ul><li>Bevel Gear</li><li>Worm Gear</li><li>Hypoid Gear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial right angle gearbox market types include bevel gear, worm gear, and hypoid gear. Bevel gears have cone-shaped teeth and are used to transmit power between intersecting shafts. Worm gears consist of a worm screw and a worm wheel and are used for high reduction ratios and self-locking capabilities. Hypoid gears have offset axes and are ideal for high torque applications. These gear types are commonly used in various industrial applications, providing efficient power transmission and facilitating the movement of machinery and equipment.</p></p>
