<p><p>Sorbitol Powder Market Types refer to different forms of sorbitol powder available in the market. Pan-agglomerated Sorbitol is a type where the sorbitol particles are formed by agglomerating smaller particles together. This process results in larger particle size and improved flowability. Spray-dried Sorbitol, on the other hand, is obtained by subjecting sorbitol solution to a spray drying process, resulting in a fine powder.
