<p><strong>The XRF Analysers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wavelength Dispersive (WDXRF)</li><li>Energy Dispersive (EDXRF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>XRF analyzers are widely used in various industries for material analysis. There are two main types of XRF analyzers: Wavelength Dispersive (WDXRF) and Energy Dispersive (EDXRF) analyzers.</p><p>WDXRF analyzers utilize a diffraction grating to disperse X-rays based on their wavelength, allowing for precise elemental analysis of a sample. On the other hand, EDXRF analyzers use a solid-state detector to measure the energy of X-rays emitted from a sample, providing rapid and efficient analysis.</p><p>Both types offer unique advantages and are chosen depending on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
