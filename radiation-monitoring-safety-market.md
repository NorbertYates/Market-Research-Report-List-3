<p><p>Radiation monitoring safety market comprises various types of technologies to detect and measure radiation levels. Scintillators are widely used materials that emit light when exposed to radiation, which is then measured to determine radiation levels. Silicon photomultipliers use arrays of photodiodes to convert radiation into electric current. Avalanche photodiodes detect radiation by multiplying the charge carriers through impact ionization. PIN diodes work by generating an electric current when exposed to radiation. Other technologies include solid-state detectors, gas-filled detectors, and neutron detectors, among others, each with their unique mechanisms for monitoring radiation safely.</p></p>
