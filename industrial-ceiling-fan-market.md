<p><p>The industrial ceiling fan market can be classified into two main types: AC ceiling fans and DC ceiling fans. AC ceiling fans are powered by alternating current and are commonly used in industrial settings. They are known for their powerful airflow and high speed. On the other hand, DC ceiling fans are powered by direct current and are more energy-efficient and quieter compared to AC fans. They are gaining popularity in industrial applications due to their lower energy consumption and ability to be powered by renewable energy sources like solar panels.</p></p>
