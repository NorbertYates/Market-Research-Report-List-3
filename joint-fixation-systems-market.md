<p><p>Joint Fixation Systems Market includes various types of devices such as plates, screws, rods, pins, and wires used in orthopedic surgeries to stabilize and fixate fractured bones or joints. Plates are flat metal implants used to bridge and hold bone fragments together. Screws are threaded implants used to secure plates or fix bones in place. Rods are inserted into the bone to provide support and stability. Pins are thin metal implants used to hold bones together, while wires are used for temporary fixation or in conjunction with other devices.</p></p>
