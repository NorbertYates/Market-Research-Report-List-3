<p><strong>The Air Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air-Release Valves</li><li>Air/Vacuum Valves</li><li>Combination Valves</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Air valves are essential components in water supply systems to control the flow of air and prevent issues like water hammer or vacuum conditions. Air-release valves release air from the system during operation, ensuring optimal performance and avoiding potential damage. Air/vacuum valves perform the dual function of releasing air during system filling and allowing air to enter when draining, preventing vacuum formation. Combination valves integrate air release and air/vacuum valve functions into a single unit, providing comprehensive air control solutions. These valves are crucial in maintaining water supply network efficiency and avoiding system malfunctions.</p></p>
