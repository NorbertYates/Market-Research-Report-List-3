<p><p>The flat glass processing machines market is categorized into three main types: tempered processing machines, laminated and insulating glass processing machines, and coated processing machines. Tempered processing machines are used to strengthen glass by heating and cooling it rapidly, making it more resistant to breakage. Laminated and insulating glass processing machines are used to create multiple layers of glass with interlayers for added strength and insulation. Coated processing machines apply various coatings to glass surfaces, such as low-emissivity coatings for energy efficiency or reflective coatings for aesthetics.</p></p>
