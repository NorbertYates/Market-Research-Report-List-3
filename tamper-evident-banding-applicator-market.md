There are two main types of banding markets: full-body banding and neck banding. </p><p>Full-body banding involves applying a band around the entire product, providing overall tamper-evident protection. This is commonly used for products like beverages, canned goods, and personal care items. </p><p>Neck banding, on the other hand, involves applying a band only around the neck or cap of a product. This type of banding is often used for items such as pharmaceuticals, condiments, and cosmetics. Both methods ensure product integrity and security during distribution and retail sale.</p></p>
