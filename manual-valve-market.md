<p><p>The manual valve market consists of various types including gate valves, control valves, ball valves, and butterfly valves. Gate valves are used to control the flow of fluids by lifting or lowering a gate mechanism. Control valves regulate the flow rate or pressure of fluids in a system. Ball valves have a rotating ball with a hole that controls the flow of fluids. Butterfly valves have a circular disc that pivots to control the flow. These valves are manually operated and find applications in various industries.</p></p>
