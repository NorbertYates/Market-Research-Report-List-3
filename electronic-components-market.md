<p><p>The electronic components market can be categorized into three main types: active components, passive components, and electromechanical components. Active components, such as transistors and integrated circuits, can control the flow of electrical signals and are capable of amplification or conversion. Passive components, like resistors and capacitors, do not require external power and can store, dissipate, or regulate energy. Electromechanical components, such as switches and relays, combine electrical and mechanical functions to control power or signal flow. These different types of components play essential roles in various electronic systems and devices.</p></p>
