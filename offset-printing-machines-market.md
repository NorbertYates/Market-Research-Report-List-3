<p><p>Offset printing machines are commonly categorized into two types: web offset printing machines and sheet-fed offset printing machines. Web offset printing machines are used for printing large volumes of publications, such as newspapers, magazines, and catalogs. They use continuous rolls of paper, making the printing process more efficient and cost-effective. On the other hand, sheet-fed offset printing machines are used for smaller runs and specialized printing, such as packaging, labels, and brochures. They utilize individual sheets of paper, allowing for more flexibility and customization in the printing process.</p></p>
