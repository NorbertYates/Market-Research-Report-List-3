<p><p>Dispenser controller refers to a device used to manage the flow of fluids or gases in various settings. There are two main types of dispenser controllers available in the market. The compensation type dispenser controller includes features that automatically adjust the dispensing flow rate to compensate for external factors such as pressure changes, ensuring accurate and precise dispensing.
