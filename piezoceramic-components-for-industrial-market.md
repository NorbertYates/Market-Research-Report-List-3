<p><p>Piezoceramic components are widely used in the industrial market for various applications. There are three main types of piezoceramic components: PZT-based, PMN-based, and others. PZT-based components are composed of lead zirconate titanate and are known for their high reliability and versatility, making them suitable for ultrasonic sensors, actuators, and transducers. PMN-based components utilize lead magnesium niobate and offer excellent piezoelectric properties, making them ideal for applications requiring high performance and precision, such as piezoelectric motors and medical devices. The "others" category includes alternative materials and compositions that have emerged as alternatives to PZT and PMN, offering unique characteristics for specific industrial applications.</p></p>
