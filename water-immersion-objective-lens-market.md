<p><p>The water immersion objective lens market consists of different types, namely Type N, Type B, Type F, and Others. Type N lenses are designed to correct spherical aberration and are commonly used in fluorescence imaging. Type B lenses are used for brightfield imaging and require a coverslip for proper focusing. Type F lenses are specialized for live cell imaging and feature long working distances. Other types may include unique specifications not fitting into the aforementioned categories. These different market types cater to specific applications and imaging requirements within the field of microscopy.</p></p>
