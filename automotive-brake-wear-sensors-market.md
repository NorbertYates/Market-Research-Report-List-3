<p><p>Automotive Brake Wear Sensors Market consists of two main types: Electronic Brake Wear Sensors and Disc Brake Wear Sensors. Electronic Brake Wear Sensors are integrated into the brake pads and alert the driver when the pads need replacement by sending a signal to the dashboard. Disc Brake Wear Sensors are attached to the brake caliper and monitor the thickness of the brake pads, providing an indication of wear.
