<p><p>The Specialty Label Adhesive Market includes two main types: Water-based Label Adhesive and Solvent-based Label Adhesive. Water-based Label Adhesive is made with water-soluble resins and is considered more environmentally friendly and safer to use. Solvent-based Label Adhesive, on the other hand, is made with chemicals that evaporate to form a strong bond. While Solvent-based Label Adhesive may offer faster drying times and superior adhesion, it can be more harmful to the environment and user health compared to Water-based Label Adhesive.</p></p>
