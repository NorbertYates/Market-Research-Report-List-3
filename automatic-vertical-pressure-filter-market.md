<p><p>The automatic vertical pressure filter market consists of two main types: fully automatic and semiautomatic. </p><p>Fully automatic filters are equipped with advanced technology that allows them to operate independently without human intervention. They have automated processes for filter cloth cleaning, cake discharge, and liquid drainage. This type of filter provides efficient and hassle-free operation, making it suitable for larger industrial applications.</p><p>On the other hand, semiautomatic filters require some manual assistance during their operation. While they still have automated features for certain processes, such as filter cloth cleaning, they need occasional human intervention for cake discharge and liquid drainage. These filters are commonly used in smaller-scale applications where operator attention is available.</p></p>
