<p><ul><li>Mechanical Rotating Lidar</li><li>Hybrid Solid-state Lidar</li><li>Solid State Lidar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LiDAR technology in the automotive market comes in various types including mechanical rotating LiDAR, hybrid solid-state LiDAR, and solid-state LiDAR. Mechanical rotating LiDAR uses a spinning mechanism to emit and detect laser pulses, offering a high level of accuracy. Hybrid solid-state LiDAR combines the benefits of both mechanical rotating and solid-state LiDAR, providing a balance between performance and cost. Solid-state LiDAR is the latest advancement in LiDAR technology, using a fixed array of lasers to achieve high-resolution imaging without moving parts.</p></p>
