They typically come in different sizes and designs to fit different CPU sockets. On the other hand, CPU heatsinks are passive cooling solutions that absorb and dissipate heat generated by the CPU. They usually consist of metallic fins that increase the surface area for better heat dissipation. Both CPU fans and heatsinks play a crucial role in maintaining optimal temperature levels for smooth computer operation.</p></p>
