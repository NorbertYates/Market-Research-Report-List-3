<p><p>The wireless curling iron market can be classified into two main types: automatic and manual. Automatic curling irons are designed with built-in rotating barrels that automatically wrap the hair around the barrel, eliminating the need for manual wrapping. These irons usually come with adjustable temperature settings and timers for convenience. On the other hand, manual curling irons require users to manually wrap the hair around the barrel and hold it in place. They often have fixed temperature settings and are typically more affordable than automatic ones.</p></p>
