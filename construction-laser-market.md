<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The construction laser market consists of three main types of lasers: laser level, laser scan, and laser tracker. A laser level is used for straight horizontal or vertical alignment, making it ideal for tasks such as installing cabinets or laying tiles. Laser scan technology uses rotating lasers to capture detailed 3D measurements of a construction site, aiding in planning and design. Laser trackers are highly accurate devices used to measure large objects or structures, ensuring precise alignment and construction. These three types of construction lasers serve different purposes but all contribute to improving accuracy and efficiency in the construction industry.</p></p>
