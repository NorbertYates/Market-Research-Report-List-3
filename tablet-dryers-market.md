<p><ul><li>Tray Dryers</li><li>Belt Dryers</li><li>Vacuum Dryers</li><li>Spray Dryers</li><li>Rotary Dryers</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tablet dryers are utilized in the pharmaceutical industry for drying tablets and granules. Tray dryers consist of multiple trays where tablets are placed for drying. Belt dryers involve a continuous process where tablets are placed on a moving belt for drying. Vacuum dryers utilize a vacuum to accelerate the drying process. Spray dryers use hot air to evaporate the moisture from tablets. Rotary dryers employ a rotating drum to dry tablets. Other types of tablet dryers may include fluid bed dryers or freeze dryers, which use different techniques for drying tablets and granules.</p></p>
