<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The advanced semiconductor photomask market encompasses three types: Quartz Mask, Soda Mask, and Relief Plate. A Quartz Mask is made from synthetic quartz, providing excellent transparency and precision in the creation of photolithography patterns. A Soda Mask, on the other hand, is manufactured using soda lime glass and ensures efficient mass production of photomasks. Lastly, a Relief Plate is a three-dimensional, high-resolution mask used for specific applications, enabling precise pattern transfer. These photomask types play crucial roles in the semiconductor industry, enabling the development of smaller and more intricate electronic components.</p></p>
