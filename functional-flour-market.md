<p><p>Functional flour market includes various types such as pre-cooked flour and specialty flour. Pre-cooked flour is processed through heat treatment to improve digestibility and functional properties. It is commonly used in instant food products. Specialty flour, on the other hand, is produced from unique ingredients like ancient grains, legumes, or gluten-free sources. It caters to specific dietary needs or preferences and offers enhanced nutritional value. Both types of functional flour cater to the growing demand for convenient, healthy, and specialized food products.</p></p>
