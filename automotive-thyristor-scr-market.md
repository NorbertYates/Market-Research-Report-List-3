<p><p>Unidirectional Thyristors are designed to allow current flow in only one direction, making them suitable for applications where power is required to flow in a single direction. On the other hand, Bidirectional Thyristors are capable of conducting current in both directions, allowing for bidirectional power control. Both types of Thyristors have their own unique applications and benefits, with Unidirectional Thyristors being commonly used in rectifier circuits and Bidirectional Thyristors being used in applications such as motor control and AC power control.</p></p>
