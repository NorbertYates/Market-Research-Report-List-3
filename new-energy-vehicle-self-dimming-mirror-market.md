<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle Self-dimming Mirror market includes two main types: Outer Self-dimming Mirror and Inside Self-dimming Mirror. Outer mirrors are equipped with sensors that automatically dim in response to headlights from other vehicles to reduce glare for the driver. Inside mirrors use electrochromic technology to dim the mirror based on ambient light conditions. Both types offer enhanced safety and visibility for drivers, making them popular choices in the market for New Energy Vehicles.</p></p>
