<p><p>Electric upsetting machines are used for metal forming processes, and are available in different market types including vertical and horizontal machines. Vertical electric upsetting machines are designed with a vertical orientation, allowing for the formation of metal parts by pressing them against a fixed die. In contrast, horizontal electric upsetting machines have a horizontal orientation, where the metal parts are compressed between a moving die and a stationary anvil. Both types of machines have their unique advantages and applications, depending on the specific metal forming requirements of the industry.</p></p>
