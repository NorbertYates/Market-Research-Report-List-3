<p><p>The heavy-duty bearing market consists of two major types: ball bearings and roller bearings. Ball bearings are comprised of several balls inside a ring, enabling smooth and efficient rotational movement. They are commonly used in applications with lighter loads and high-speed requirements. On the other hand, roller bearings use cylindrical or tapered rollers to carry heavier loads and withstand greater pressures. These bearings are more suitable for applications that involve heavy machinery such as construction equipment, mining machinery, and industrial machinery.</p></p>
