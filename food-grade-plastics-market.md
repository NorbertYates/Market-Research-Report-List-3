<p><p>Food Grade Plastics Market Types refer to different types of plastics that are safe for food packaging and storage. High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE) is known for its high strength and resistance to chemicals. Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE) is flexible and commonly used in squeeze bottles and food wraps. Polycarbonate (PC) is durable and clear, often used in baby bottles and water bottles. Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) is lightweight and commonly used in beverage bottles. Polypropylene (PP) is heat-resistant and used in microwave-safe containers. Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene (ABS) is strong and durable, often used in food storage containers.</p></p>
