<p><p>The automotive bicycle rack market encompasses various types designed to transport bicycles on vehicles. Hitch mounted racks attach to the back of vehicles, providing easy access and visibility. Roof mounted racks are installed on the roof of vehicles, offering a space-efficient solution. Trunk mounted racks are attached to the trunk or hatch of vehicles, suitable for compact cars. Pickup carriers are designed for trucks, allowing bikes to be transported in the bed of the vehicle. These different types cater to various preferences and needs of cyclists.</p></p>
