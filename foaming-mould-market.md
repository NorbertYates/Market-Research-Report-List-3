<p><p>Foaming mould, also known as foam molding or foam injection molding, is a manufacturing process used to produce lightweight and complex-shaped products using a variety of foaming materials. This foam expands and solidifies, taking the shape of the mold, and ultimately forming the desired product.</p><p>The foaming mould market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for lightweight and durable products across various industries, including automotive, aerospace, packaging, and construction. <p><p>The foaming mould market consists of various types, such as EPS (expanded polystyrene) foaming mould, EPP (expanded polypropylene) foaming mould, and PU (polyurethane) foaming mould. EPS foaming moulds are commonly used in packaging and insulation applications due to their lightweight and excellent thermal insulation properties. EPP foaming moulds are highly durable and impact-resistant, making them suitable for automotive parts and protective packaging. PU foaming moulds are versatile and used in various industries, including furniture, footwear, and automotive, due to their flexibility, comfort, and durability.
