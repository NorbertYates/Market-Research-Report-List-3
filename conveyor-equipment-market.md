<p><p>The conveyor equipment market consists of various types of conveyors, including roller conveyors, belt conveyors, overhead conveyors, pallet conveyors, and other conveyors. Roller conveyors use rollers to transport materials or products. Belt conveyors use belts on a continuous loop to move items. Overhead conveyors are suspended from ceilings and move items along a track. Pallet conveyors are designed specifically for the transportation of pallets. Other conveyors include screw conveyors, chain conveyors, and slat conveyors, each serving different purposes in material handling and transportation.</p></p>
