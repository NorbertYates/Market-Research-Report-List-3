<p><p>Smart bridges market can be categorized into hardware, accelerometers, anemometers, and other types of sensors. Hardware includes physical components like sensors and cameras that monitor the bridge's condition. Accelerometers detect movements and vibrations of the bridge structure. Anemometers measure wind speed and direction, which can have an impact on the bridge's stability. Other sensors may include temperature gauges, humidity sensors, and corrosion detectors, providing a comprehensive overview of the bridge's health and performance.</p></p>
