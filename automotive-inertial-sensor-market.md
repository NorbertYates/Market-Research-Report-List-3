<p><p>Automotive Inertial Sensor Market consists of two main types: accelerometers and gyroscopes. Accelerometers measure the rate of change of velocity and can detect changes in the speed and direction of the vehicle. Gyroscopes, on the other hand, measure the angular rate of rotation and can detect the orientation of the vehicle. These sensors are crucial for ensuring stability and safety in automotive applications by providing real-time data on the vehicle's movement and position. They are essential components in various automotive systems such as electronic stability control and adaptive cruise control.</p></p>
