<p><p>ICP spectrometers are analytical instruments used in various industries for elemental analysis. There are two main types of ICP spectrometers: sequential type and simultaneous type. </p><p>Sequential type ICP spectrometers analyze elements one at a time by altering the wavelength of the light emitted by the plasma. This provides high accuracy and sensitivity for elemental analysis, making it suitable for demanding applications.</p><p>Simultaneous type ICP spectrometers use an array of detectors to simultaneously measure multiple wavelengths emitted by the plasma. This allows for rapid analysis of multiple elements, enhancing productivity and efficiency in various analytical processes.</p></p>
