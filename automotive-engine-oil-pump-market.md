<p><ul><li>Mechanical Oil Pump</li><li>Electric Oil Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive engine oil pump market consists of two main types: mechanical oil pumps and electric oil pumps. Mechanical oil pumps are driven by the engine's crankshaft through a series of gears, providing consistent oil pressure and flow to lubricate engine components. On the other hand, electric oil pumps are powered by the vehicle's electrical system, offering more precise control over oil pressure and flow. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring proper lubrication and cooling of the engine, ultimately enhancing its performance and longevity.</p></p>
