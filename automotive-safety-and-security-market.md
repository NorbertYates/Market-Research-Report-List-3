<p><p>Active safety in the automotive industry refers to technologies and systems that help prevent accidents from occurring by actively intervening in the driving process. This includes features such as automatic emergency braking, lane departure warning, and adaptive cruise control. On the other hand, passive safety measures are designed to protect occupants in the event of a collision, such as airbags, seatbelts, and crumple zones. Both active and passive safety technologies work together to provide comprehensive protection for drivers and passengers on the road.</p></p>
