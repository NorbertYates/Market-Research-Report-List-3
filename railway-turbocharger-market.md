<p><p>Railway turbocharger market is divided into two main types based on the type of engine they are designed for - two-stroke engine and four-stroke engine. Two-stroke engine turbochargers are smaller and simpler in design, making them ideal for smaller railway engines. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the efficiency and performance of railway engines.</p></p>
