<p><p>Laminated Strand Lumber (LSL) is a type of engineered wood product used in construction. There are two main market types for LSL: structural and non-structural. Structural LSL is used in applications where strength and load-bearing capacity are important, such as in beams, headers, and studs. Non-structural LSL is used for applications where strength is not the primary concern, such as in sheathing, subflooring, and furniture. Both types of LSL offer a durable and cost-effective alternative to traditional lumber products.</p></p>
