<p><p>AC solid state relays (SSRs) are electronic switches that can control the flow of alternating current (AC) without moving parts. They come in various types such as DIN rail mounting, panel-mount, surface-mount, and other markets. DIN rail mounting SSRs are designed to be installed on DIN rails, which are commonly used in industrial control panels. Panel-mount SSRs are directly mounted on the control panel for easy access. Surface-mount SSRs are compact and can be directly soldered onto printed circuit boards. Other markets refer to specialized applications where SSRs are used, such as motor control, heating systems, and lighting control.</p></p>
