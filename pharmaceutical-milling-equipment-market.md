<p><p>Pharmaceutical milling equipment market includes various types such as cone mills and hammer mills. Cone mills are used for achieving uniform particle size reduction and fine particles in pharmaceutical powders. They are commonly used for wet and dry granulation processes. On the other hand, hammer mills are utilized for high-speed milling and size reduction of pharmaceutical ingredients. They are known for their versatility in handling a wide range of materials and producing consistent particle size distribution. Both cone mills and hammer mills play a critical role in the pharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
