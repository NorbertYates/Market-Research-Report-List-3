<p><strong>The Automotive Drive Shaft Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Half Shaft</li><li>Propeller Shaft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive drive shaft market consists of two main types: half shaft and propeller shaft. Half shafts are used in vehicles with front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive. They connect the transaxle or differential to the front wheels, transmitting power and allowing them to turn. On the other hand, propeller shafts are found in rear-wheel drive vehicles. They transfer torque from the transmission to the differential, enabling the rotation of the rear wheels. Both types of drive shafts play crucial roles in ensuring the smooth operation and power delivery of vehicles.</p></p>
