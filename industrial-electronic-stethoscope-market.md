<p><p>Low frequency industrial electronic stethoscopes are designed to detect lower frequency sounds, typically below 200Hz, making them ideal for detecting issues in machinery, pipelines, and HVAC systems. High frequency industrial electronic stethoscopes, on the other hand, are specifically tuned to pick up higher frequency sounds, typically above 200Hz, allowing for precise detection of issues in engines, bearings, and electrical systems. Both types of stethoscopes are essential tools for maintenance technicians and engineers in various industries for accurate and efficient diagnostics.</p></p>
