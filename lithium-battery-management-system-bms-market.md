<p><strong>The Lithium Battery Management System (BMS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centralized BMS</li><li>Distributed BMS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Lithium Battery Management System (BMS) market is divided into two main types: Centralized BMS and Distributed BMS. Centralized BMS involves the use of a single control unit for managing the entire battery system. It offers a centralized approach to monitoring and controlling battery performance. On the other hand, Distributed BMS consists of multiple control units placed throughout the battery pack, allowing for distributed monitoring and control. This provides increased flexibility and redundancy in managing battery operations. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in battery management.</p></p>
