<p><p>Petroleum refining catalysts can be categorized into three main types: chemicals, zeolites, and metals. Chemical catalysts are synthetic substances that help speed up chemical reactions in the refining process. Zeolites are crystalline minerals that act as molecular sieves, selectively trapping certain molecules during the refining process. Metals like platinum, palladium, and nickel are used as catalysts due to their ability to facilitate reactions at lower temperatures and pressures. Each type of catalyst plays a critical role in optimizing the efficiency of petroleum refining processes.</p></p>
