<p><p>The plastic bottle filling machine market can be categorized into three types: fully automatic, semi-automatic, and manual. Fully automatic machines require minimal human intervention as they automatically perform all the processes, from bottle loading to filling and capping. Semi-automatic machines involve some level of human involvement, such as bottle positioning and capping. Manual machines require complete human interaction throughout the entire filling process. These different market types offer varying levels of efficiency, production capacity, and cost-effectiveness, allowing businesses to choose the most suitable option based on their needs and resources.</p></p>
