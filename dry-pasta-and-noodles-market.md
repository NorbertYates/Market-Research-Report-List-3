<p><strong>The Dry Pasta and Noodles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Durum Wheat Semolina Made</li><li>Soft Wheat Flour Made</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dry pasta and noodles market can be classified into two types: durum wheat semolina made and soft wheat flour made. Durum wheat semolina made pasta and noodles are made from the endosperm of durum wheat, known for its high protein and gluten content. This type of pasta and noodles have a firm texture, excellent cooking properties, and are commonly used in Italian cuisine. On the other hand, soft wheat flour made pasta and noodles are made from the endosperm of soft wheat, resulting in a softer texture and lighter taste. They are often used in Asian dishes like ramen and udon.</p></p>
