<p><p>Tinted soft contact lenses are available in different market types, namely visibility tint, enhancement tint, and opaque tint. Visibility tint lenses have a faint color that helps users locate the lens easily if dropped. Enhancement tint lenses enhance the natural eye color by adding a subtle tint. Opaque tint lenses completely change the eye color, regardless of the user's natural eye color. Each market type serves a specific purpose, providing options to users in terms of convenience, aesthetic enhancement, and eye color alteration.</p></p>
