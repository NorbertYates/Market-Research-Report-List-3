<p><p>Pneumatic tube transport systems are divided into two market types: fully automatic and semi-automatic. In a fully automatic system, the entire process from loading the materials to the delivery destination is automatically controlled and managed. This eliminates the need for human intervention throughout the transport process. On the other hand, a semi-automatic system requires some level of human intervention, such as loading the materials into the tube or overseeing the operation. These market types offer different levels of automation and efficiency to cater to varying needs in the transport industry.</p></p>
