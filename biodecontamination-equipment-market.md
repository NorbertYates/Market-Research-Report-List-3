<p><p>Biodecontamination equipment market includes both chamber decontamination and room decontamination types. Chamber decontamination involves the use of specialized equipment to eliminate contaminants present in enclosed spaces, such as laboratories or cleanrooms. This process ensures a controlled environment free from harmful substances. On the other hand, room decontamination is a broader approach that targets larger areas, such as entire rooms or buildings, to remove contaminants. Both types of biodecontamination equipment play a vital role in maintaining hygiene and safety standards in various industries, including healthcare, pharmaceuticals, and research facilities.</p></p>
