<p><p>Sealants market consists of various types including water-borne, solvent-borne, reactive, and hot-melts. Water-borne sealants are environmentally friendly and easy to clean up. Solvent-borne sealants are cost-effective but may release harmful chemicals. Reactive sealants cure by chemical reaction and offer excellent adhesion. Hot-melts sealants are heated to apply and solidify upon cooling. Each type caters to different needs and preferences, providing options for various industries and applications within the sealants market.</p></p>
