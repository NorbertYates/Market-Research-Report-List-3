PIR sensors detect heat radiated by human bodies, objects, or animals and are commonly used in security systems. They are cost-effective and have high energy efficiency. On the other hand, Imaging Infrared Sensors create detailed images using infrared radiation, which enables them to capture more precise information about the surrounding environment. They find applications in fields like military, aerospace, and surveillance.</p></p>
