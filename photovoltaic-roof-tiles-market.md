<p><p>The photovoltaic roof tile market is comprised of two main types: monocrystalline and polycrystalline. Monocrystalline tiles are made from a single crystal structure, resulting in higher efficiency and cost, making them the more premium option. Polycrystalline tiles are made from multiple crystal structures, yielding slightly lower efficiency and cost. Both types convert sunlight into electricity using solar cells embedded within the tiles. The choice between monocrystalline and polycrystalline tiles depends on budget and desired efficiency, with monocrystalline being more efficient but also more expensive.</p></p>
