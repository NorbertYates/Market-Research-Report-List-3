<p><ul><li>Instantaneous Steam Water Heater</li><li>Semi-instantaneous Steam Water Heater</li><li>Storage Steam Water Heater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial water heaters are classified into three main categories: Instantaneous Steam Water Heater, Semi-instantaneous Steam Water Heater, and Storage Steam Water Heater. Instantaneous heaters heat water on demand, providing hot water continuously. Semi-instantaneous heaters store a small amount of hot water and heat more as needed. Storage heaters store large volumes of hot water for future use. Each type caters to different industrial needs, offering various benefits such as energy efficiency, space savings, and high productivity in different industrial settings.</p></p>
