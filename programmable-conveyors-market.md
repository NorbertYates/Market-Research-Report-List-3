<p><p>The programmable conveyors market includes various types such as Automated Guided Vehicles (AGV) systems, Monorail systems, and Powered Roller systems. AGV systems consist of vehicles that are guided by pre-set programming, suitable for transportation tasks in warehouses and factories. Monorail systems utilize a single rail to move objects and are commonly used for heavy loads. Powered Roller systems use motorized rollers to transport items, offering a flexible and efficient solution for moving goods in a wide range of industries.</p></p>
