<p><p>Silicone crosslinked polyethylene is available in three main market types: extrusion grade, injection molding grade, and stripping grade. Extrusion grade is specifically designed for processes where the material is forced through a die to create a specific shape or form. Injection molding grade is used to produce intricate and complex shapes through injecting molten material into a mold. Stripping grade is designed for applications where the material needs to be easily removed or stripped away from a surface.</p></p>
