<p><p>The Automotive Digital Mirror market consists of two main types: Interior Digital Mirror and Outside Digital Mirror. Interior Digital Mirrors are integrated into the interior of the vehicle, providing drivers with a clear view of their surroundings without obstructing their line of sight. Outside Digital Mirrors are located on the exterior of the vehicle and use cameras to display real-time images of the vehicle's surroundings to the driver. Both types offer improved visibility and safety features compared to traditional mirrors.</p></p>
