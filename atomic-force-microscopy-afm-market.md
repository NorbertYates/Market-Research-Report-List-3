<p><p>Atomic Force Microscopy (AFM) is a powerful tool used for imaging surfaces with high resolution. There are different types of AFM markets including Contact AFM, where the probe physically touches the sample; Non-contact AFM, where the probe hovers above the sample; Dynamic Contact AFM, where the probe oscillates while in contact with the sample; and Tapping AFM, where the probe taps the sample surface. Each type offers unique advantages and applications in the field of nanotechnology and materials science.</p></p>
