<p><p>Rock dusters are used in mining operations to minimize the risk of explosions by dispersing fine particles of inert material, such as limestone or clay, to control the accumulation of coal dust. There are two main types of rock duster markets: hydraulic and electric. Hydraulic rock dusters utilize water pressure to disperse the inert material, while electric rock dusters employ electric motors to propel and disperse the particles. Both market types aim to enhance safety in mining operations by preventing the hazardous accumulation of coal dust.</p></p>
