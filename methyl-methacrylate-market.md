<p><p>The Methyl Methacrylate (MMA) market is classified into three types based on production methods: ACH method, Isobutylene method, and Ethylene method. In the ACH method, MMA is produced through acetone cyanohydrin as an intermediate compound. The Isobutylene method utilizes isobutylene as a raw material to produce MMA. Lastly, the Ethylene method involves the conversion of ethylene into MMA. These three methods are commonly used in the market to manufacture Methyl Methacrylate, catering to various industrial applications.</p></p>
