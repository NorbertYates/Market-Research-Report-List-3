Simultaneous thermal analyzers combine multiple techniques in one instrument, and differential thermal analyzers measure temperature changes. Thermomechanical analyzers measure dimensional changes, dynamic mechanical analyzers measure mechanical properties, and there are other types of thermal analysis equipment available as well. These instruments play a crucial role in industries such as materials science, pharmaceuticals, and polymers.</p></p>
