<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The parts washer market can be divided into two types: solvent-based and water/aqueous-based. Solvent-based parts washers use a solvent as a cleaning agent to remove grease, oil, and other contaminants from parts. These machines are effective in heavy-duty cleaning but may be hazardous to the environment and human health. On the other hand, water/aqueous-based parts washers use water-based detergents to clean parts, making them safer and eco-friendly. They are suitable for lighter cleaning tasks and are increasingly preferred due to stricter regulations on solvent usage.</p></p>
