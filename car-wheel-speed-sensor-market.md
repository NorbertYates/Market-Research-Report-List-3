<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Car Wheel Speed Sensor Market consists of two main types: Hall Type and Magnetic Electric Type. </p><p>Hall Type sensors use a Hall effect device to measure changes in magnetic fields generated by rotating wheels, providing accurate speed and direction data. On the other hand, Magnetic Electric Type sensors use magnets and coils to detect wheel movement and determine speed. These sensors are essential components in modern vehicles, providing crucial information to the vehicle's anti-lock braking system and traction control system for safe and efficient driving.</p></p>
