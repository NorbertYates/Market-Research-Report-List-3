<p><p>The popcorn market can be classified into two main types: ready-to-eat popcorn and microwave popcorn. Ready-to-eat popcorn refers to the pre-popped popcorn that is packaged and ready to consume immediately. It is popular among consumers seeking convenience and a quick snack option. On the other hand, microwave popcorn is designed to be cooked in a microwave oven. It typically comes in a bag or a container with pre-measured kernels and seasoning, allowing people to make fresh popcorn at home easily. Both types cater to consumers' different needs for instant popcorn enjoyment.</p></p>
