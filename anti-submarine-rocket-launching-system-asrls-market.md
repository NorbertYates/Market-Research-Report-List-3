<p><p>The Anti Submarine Rocket Launching System (ASRLS) market is segmented into three types: hot launch, cold launch, and concentric canister launch. </p><p>In a hot launch system, the rocket is ignited inside the launch tube and then ejected, providing immediate propulsion. Cold launch systems, on the other hand, eject the rocket first and then ignite it after a safe distance, ensuring protection of the launching platform. Lastly, concentric canister launch systems involve utilizing multiple concentric canisters to launch rockets simultaneously, resulting in increased launch capability and efficiency.</p></p>
