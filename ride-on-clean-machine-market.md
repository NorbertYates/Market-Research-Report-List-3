<p><p>The ride-on clean machine market consists of two main types: ride-on sweeper and ride-on scrubber dryer. Ride-on sweepers are designed to clean large outdoor areas such as parking lots and warehouses, using rotating brushes to gather debris into a collection bin. Ride-on scrubber dryers, on the other hand, are used for indoor surfaces like warehouse floors, using a combination of scrubbing and drying functions to leave floors clean and dry. Both types of machines are designed to efficiently clean large surface areas, saving time and labor.</p></p>
