<p><ul><li>Granular Carbon Filters</li><li>Bonded Carbon Filters</li><li>Activated Carbon Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas phase carbon filters market comprises three major types: granular carbon filters, bonded carbon filters, and activated carbon filters. Granular carbon filters are made up of small carbon particles and are commonly used to remove odors and chemical contaminants. Bonded carbon filters are made by combining carbon particles with a binder, resulting in enhanced durability and efficiency. Activated carbon filters are made using carbon that has been specially treated to create a highly porous structure, allowing for greater adsorption of gases and vapors. These filters are widely used in industries such as air purification and water treatment.</p></p>
