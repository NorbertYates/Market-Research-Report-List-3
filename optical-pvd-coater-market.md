<p><p>The Optical PVD Coater market includes two main types of equipment: Evaporation Equipment and Sputtering Equipment. Evaporation equipment is used to deposit thin films on a substrate by heating the material until it vaporizes and then condenses on the substrate. Sputtering equipment, on the other hand, uses plasma to dislodge atoms from a target material, which then coat the substrate. Both types of equipment are vital in the production of various products such as semiconductors, optical coatings, and decorative finishes.</p></p>
