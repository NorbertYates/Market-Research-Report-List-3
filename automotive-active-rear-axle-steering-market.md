<p><p>Automotive Active Rear Axle Steering technology includes two main types: Single Actuator and Dual Actuator systems. </p><p>The Single Actuator system utilizes one actuator to control the rear axle steering, providing simplified control and lower cost compared to Dual Actuator systems. In contrast, Dual Actuator systems feature two separate actuators for each rear wheel, allowing for more precise and dynamic control of the rear axle steering. Both types offer improved maneuverability and stability, enhancing the overall driving experience in vehicles equipped with this technology.</p></p>
