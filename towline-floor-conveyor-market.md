<p><ul><li>On-floor Towline Conveyor</li><li>In-floor Towline Conveyor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Towline floor conveyors are used to transport materials within a facility. There are two types of towline floor conveyor markets: on-floor towline conveyor and in-floor towline conveyor. On-floor towline conveyor systems are installed on the shop floor and often consist of a painted line or track where the materials are pulled. In-floor towline conveyor systems are concealed within the floor, with a track buried underground. Both types of towline floor conveyors are used in warehouses, manufacturing plants, and other industries to efficiently move materials from one location to another.</p></p>
