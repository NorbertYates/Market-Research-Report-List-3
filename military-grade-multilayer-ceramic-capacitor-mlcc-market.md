<p><p>The Military Grade Multilayer Ceramic Capacitor (MLCC) market is segmented into two types: PME MLCC and BME MLCC. PME MLCC refers to Passive Metalized Electrode MLCC, which has a metalized electrode layer to improve electrical performance. BME MLCC, on the other hand, stands for Base Metal Electrode MLCC, where a base metal electrode layer is used instead of precious metals, reducing costs without compromising performance. Both types are widely used in military applications due to their high reliability, durability, and ability to withstand harsh environments.</p></p>
