<p><p>The industrial duct exhaust muffler market consists of different types of mufflers. Resistive mufflers reduce noise by creating resistance in the airflow. Resistant mufflers are designed to withstand harsh industrial environments. Impedance composite mufflers use layers of materials to absorb sound. Micro perforated plate mufflers use small holes to dissipate noise. Small hole mufflers employ tiny openings to attenuate sound. Active mufflers utilize advanced technology to actively cancel out noise. These mufflers cater to various industrial needs by providing effective noise reduction and ensuring durability in different working conditions.</p></p>
