GH refers to the Grounding Hubs used in lightning protection systems to ground electrical currents safely. GFL represents Grounding and Fault Locator devices used to locate and identify faults in lightning protection systems. GJT stands for Grounding Jumper Towers, which are used to connect lightning rods to grounding systems. GFW stands for Grounding Wire systems used to protect buildings and structures against lightning strikes. Lastly, the "Others" category includes additional lightning rod tower market segments not mentioned above.</p></p>
