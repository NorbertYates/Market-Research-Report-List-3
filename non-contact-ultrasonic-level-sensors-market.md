<p><strong>The Non Contact Ultrasonic Level Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Switch Type</li><li>Continuous Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-contact ultrasonic level sensors are divided into two main market types - switch type and continuous type. Switch type sensors are designed to detect the presence or absence of material at a specific level, triggering an alarm or control action. Continuous type sensors, on the other hand, provide real-time, continuous level measurement data. Switch type sensors are typically used in simpler applications where only discrete level detection is needed, while continuous type sensors are utilized in more complex processes that require precise monitoring and control.</p></p>
