<p><p>Unit sorters are advanced technologies used to automate the process of sorting items in warehouses and distribution centers. Cross-belt sorters are high-speed conveyor systems that use belts to transfer items to different chutes for sorting. Tilt tray sorters are conveyor systems with trays that tilt to drop items into designated chutes. Both technologies are increasingly popular in the market due to their efficiency and accuracy in sorting a wide range of items quickly. These sorter types are essential for companies looking to streamline their operations and improve productivity.</p></p>
