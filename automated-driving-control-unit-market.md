<p><strong>The Automated Driving Control Unit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Perception Control Unit</li><li>Decision Control Unit</li><li>Actuator Control Unit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automated Driving Control Unit market consists of three main types: Perception Control Unit, Decision Control Unit, and Actuator Control Unit. The Perception Control Unit uses sensors and cameras to gather data about the vehicle's surroundings. The Decision Control Unit processes this data and makes decisions based on it, such as when to accelerate or brake. The Actuator Control Unit then translates these decisions into actions, such as turning the steering wheel or applying the brakes. These three components work together to enable safe and autonomous driving.</p></p>
