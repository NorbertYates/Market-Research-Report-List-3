<p><strong>The Automotive Ethernet Connector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The Automotive Ethernet Connector market consists of two main types: Common Connectors and High-Speed Connectors. Common Connectors are used in different automotive systems such as infotainment, navigation, and body control, offering low to medium speeds (up to 100 Mbps) for data transmission. On the other hand, High-Speed Connectors are designed specifically for more demanding applications like advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS) and autonomous driving. They provide faster data transmission (up to 1 Gbps) to support the high-bandwidth requirements of these systems.</p></p>
