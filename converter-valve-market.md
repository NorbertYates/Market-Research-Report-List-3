<p><p>The Converter Valve Market encompasses three main types: Ultra High Voltage (UHV), Extra High Voltage (EHV), and High Voltage (HV). UHV converter valves are designed for extremely high voltages, typically above 800 kV, providing efficient transmission over long distances. EHV converter valves operate at voltages between 300 kV and 800 kV, ensuring reliable power transmission across large areas. HV converter valves, with voltages ranging from 60 kV to 300 kV, facilitate electricity transmission within a region. These market segments cater to specific voltage requirements for efficient power transmission on various scales.</p></p>
