<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cation exchange resins market is categorized into two main types - Type I and Type II. Type I resins have a higher cross-linking degree which results in higher selectivity for certain cations. They are often used in applications where high purity is required, such as pharmaceuticals and food processing. On the other hand, Type II resins have a lower cross-linking degree, providing faster kinetics and higher capacity. They are commonly used in water treatment and industrial processes where high flow rates are necessary.</p></p>
