<p><p>The Electro-Static Discharge (ESD) Tape market comprises two types: Single Side Tape and Double Sides Tape. Single Side Tape refers to ESD tape that has adhesive on one side only, allowing it to easily attach to surfaces while protecting against static electricity. On the other hand, Double Sides Tape is equipped with adhesive on both sides, enabling it to join two surfaces together and provide protection against electro-static discharge. These types of ESD tapes are widely used in industries dealing with electronic components to safeguard against damage caused by static electricity.</p></p>
