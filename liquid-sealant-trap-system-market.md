<p><p>Liquid sealant trap systems are used to prevent the escape of gases or liquids from pipelines and storage tanks. There are two main types of liquid sealant trap systems: Slug and Sealing. </p><p>Slug trap systems are designed to capture and contain large amounts of liquid or gas that may accidentally enter the pipeline, preventing it from reaching sensitive equipment or the environment. </p><p>Sealing trap systems, on the other hand, are used to create a tight seal around valves or other openings in the pipeline, preventing any leaks or emissions. Both types of liquid sealant trap systems play a crucial role in maintaining the integrity and safety of industrial processes.</p></p>
