<p><strong>The Underwater Monitoring System for Oil and Gas Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Frequency Division Multiple Access(FDMA)</li><li>Time Division Multiple Access(TDMA)</li><li>Code Division Multiple Access(CDMA)</li><li>Space Division Multiple Access(SFMA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Underwater monitoring systems for the oil and gas market utilize different types of access methods to transmit and receive data. Frequency Division Multiple Access (FDMA) enables multiple devices to share the same frequency band by allocating non-overlapping frequency ranges. Time Division Multiple Access (TDMA) allows devices to take turns using the same frequency by dividing it into time slots. Code Division Multiple Access (CDMA) enables simultaneous transmission by assigning specific codes to each device. Space Division Multiple Access (SFMA) allocates different physical spaces to separate devices, allowing them to coexist.</p></p>
