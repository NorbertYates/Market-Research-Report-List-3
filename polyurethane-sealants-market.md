<p><ul><li>Single Component</li><li>Multi Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyurethane sealants are commonly divided into two main types based on their formulation: single component and multi component sealants. Single component sealants are pre-mixed and ready to use, making them convenient for quick applications. On the other hand, multi component sealants require mixing two or more components before use, allowing for more precise control over curing times and properties. Both types offer excellent adhesion, flexibility, and durability, making them popular choices for sealing applications in construction, automotive, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
