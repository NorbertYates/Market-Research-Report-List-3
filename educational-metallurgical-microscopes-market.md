<p><p>The educational metallurgical microscopes market is categorized into three types: upright metallurgical microscopes, inverted metallurgical microscopes, and boom stand metallurgical microscopes. Upright metallurgical microscopes are designed with the sample placed above the objective, allowing for examination of flat, polished samples. Inverted metallurgical microscopes have the objective placed above the sample, suitable for observing samples immersed in liquid or bulk samples. Boom stand metallurgical microscopes are flexible and provide easy maneuverability, making them ideal for examining larger components and samples.</p></p>
