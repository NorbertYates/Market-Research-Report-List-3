<p><p>Acoustic windshields are designed to reduce noise and improve driving comfort. The two main types of acoustic windshields in the market are laminated glass and acoustic glass. Laminated glass consists of multiple layers of glass with a layer of acoustic interlayer between them to absorb sound waves. Acoustic glass, on the other hand, is designed with a special acoustic film that helps to dampen noise and vibrations. Both types of windshields offer excellent noise reduction properties, enhancing the overall driving experience.</p></p>
