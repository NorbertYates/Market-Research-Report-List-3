<p><p>The flat glass processing equipment market comprises various types such as float glass, tempered glass, laminated glass, coated glass, and others. Float glass involves the manufacturing of uniform, smooth glass sheets. Tempered glass is heat-treated to enhance its strength and safety properties. Laminated glass consists of multiple layers with a polymer interlayer, offering improved strength and safety. Coated glass includes a thin layer of metallic coatings to enhance insulation and energy-efficiency. The "others" category comprises additional types of glass like non-tempered, patterned, or wired glasses, catering to specific requirements and applications.</p></p>
