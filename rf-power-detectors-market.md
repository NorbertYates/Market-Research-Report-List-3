<p><ul><li>RMS Power Detectors</li><li>Non-RMS Power Detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RF Power Detectors are devices used to measure the power level of radio frequency signals. There are two main types in the market - RMS Power Detectors and Non-RMS Power Detectors. RMS Power Detectors measure the root mean square power of the RF signal, providing an accurate representation of the signal's average power. On the other hand, Non-RMS Power Detectors measure the instantaneous power levels of the RF signal without averaging, allowing for real-time power measurements. Both types serve different purposes in various applications and are chosen based on specific requirements.</p></p>
