<p><strong>The Light Truck Tyre Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Tire</li><li>Bias Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>There are two main types of light truck tires in the market - radial tires and bias tires. Radial tires have cords that run at a 90-degree angle to the direction of travel, providing better stability, fuel efficiency, and tread wear. Bias tires, on the other hand, have cords that run at a 30-45 degree angle to the direction of travel, making them more durable and better suited to off-road use. Both types have their own advantages and are popular choices among light truck owners.</p></p>
