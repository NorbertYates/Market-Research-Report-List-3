<p><p>The Two Wheelers Brake Calipers market consists of two main types: Single Piston Caliper and Multi-Piston Caliper. Single Piston Calipers have a single piston that pushes the brake pad against the rotor to slow down the wheel. On the other hand, Multi-Piston Calipers feature multiple pistons that provide more even pressure distribution, resulting in improved braking performance. Both types cater to different market needs and preferences, with Single Piston Calipers being more common in entry-level models and Multi-Piston Calipers found in high-performance bikes.</p></p>
