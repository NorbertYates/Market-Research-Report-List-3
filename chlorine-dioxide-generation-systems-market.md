<p><p>Chlorine Dioxide Generation Systems refer to the equipment used to produce chlorine dioxide, a powerful disinfectant and oxidizing agent. There are two main types of systems: Acid-Chlorite Method and Chlorine-Chlorite Method. In the Acid-Chlorite Method, chlorine dioxide is produced by reacting sodium chlorite with an acid. On the other hand, the Chlorine-Chlorite Method involves the reaction of chlorine gas with sodium chlorite to generate chlorine dioxide. Both methods have their own advantages and applications in various industries such as water treatment, food processing, and healthcare.</p></p>
