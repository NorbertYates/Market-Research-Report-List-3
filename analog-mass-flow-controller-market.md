<p><ul><li>Thermal Pressure Type</li><li>Differential Pressure Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Analog Mass Flow Controllers can be categorized into two main types based on their principle of operation: Thermal Pressure Type and Differential Pressure Type. </p><p>Thermal Pressure Type controllers measure mass flow rate by monitoring changes in temperature and pressure within the system. On the other hand, Differential Pressure Type controllers measure mass flow rate by detecting the pressure difference between two separate points in the system.</p><p>Both types of controllers are widely used in various industries for precise control of gas flow rates in different applications.</p></p>
