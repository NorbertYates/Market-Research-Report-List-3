<p><p>The hydraulic piston accumulators market can be classified into two major types: standard accumulators and high-pressure piston accumulators. Standard accumulators are commonly used for low to medium pressure applications. They store energy from a hydraulic system in the form of a compressed gas, providing a backup power source during peak demand. High-pressure piston accumulators, on the other hand, are designed to handle higher pressure levels and are used in heavy-duty applications where energy storage is required for longer durations.</p></p>
