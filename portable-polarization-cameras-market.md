<p><p>Portable polarization cameras are used to capture images and videos with specific polarization characteristics. There are two types of portable polarization cameras: fully polarized light and partially polarized light market. Fully polarized light cameras capture images and videos where the polarization is uniform across the field of view, allowing for accurate analysis of polarization properties. Partially polarized light cameras capture images and videos where the polarization is varying across the field of view, enabling the study of complex polarized light phenomena. These cameras are widely used in various applications such as remote sensing, biomedical imaging, and industrial inspection.</p></p>
