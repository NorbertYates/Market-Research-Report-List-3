The Potentiometer Type utilizes a variable resistor to measure the throttle position. The Socket Type is designed with a sensor that plugs into a socket for easy installation. The Comprehensive Type combines multiple sensors for a more accurate and reliable measurement of throttle position. Each type offers unique advantages and features to cater to different needs and preferences within the automotive industry.</p></p>
