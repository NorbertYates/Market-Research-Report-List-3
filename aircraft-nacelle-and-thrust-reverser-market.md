<p><p>An aircraft nacelle is essentially an aerodynamic enclosure that surrounds an aircraft engine. Its primary purpose is to protect the engine from external elements and provide proper airflow to maximize engine efficiency. The nacelle also helps reduce noise and enhances overall aircraft performance.</p><p>On the other hand, a thrust reverser is a mechanism installed in the nacelle that helps reduce the stopping distance of an aircraft upon landing. It redirects the engine's thrust forward, effectively slowing down the aircraft by pushing against the engine's own force.</p><p>The Aircraft Nacelle and Thrust Reverser Market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
