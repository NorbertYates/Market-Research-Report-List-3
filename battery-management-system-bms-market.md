<p><p>Battery Management System (BMS) market can be categorized into three main types based on how the system is structured: Distributed, Centralized, and Modular. Distributed BMS involves individual control units managed independently, Centralized BMS has all control functions centralized in one unit, and Modular BMS consists of multiple smaller subsystems that work together as a single unit. Each type offers unique advantages and capabilities, catering to different battery management needs in various industries and applications.</p></p>
