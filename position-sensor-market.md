<p><p>Position sensors are devices used to measure the position, displacement, or presence of an object. The photoelectric sensor market involves the use of light beams to detect and measure an object's position. Linear position sensors utilize linear motion to determine an object's position or displacement. Proximity sensors can detect the presence or absence of an object, based on its proximity to the sensor. Rotary position sensors use rotational motion to measure an object's position or angular displacement. These sensors have various applications across industries, including automotive, manufacturing, and robotics.</p></p>
