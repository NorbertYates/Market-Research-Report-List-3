<p><ul><li>Open Loop Control</li><li>Closed Loop Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Piezoelectric drivers are used in various applications such as open loop control and closed loop control. In open loop control, the driver generates a specific voltage signal to control the motion of piezoelectric devices without any feedback mechanism. This is suitable for applications where precision is not critical. On the other hand, closed loop control uses sensors to provide feedback and adjust the voltage signal accordingly, resulting in more precise and accurate control of the piezoelectric devices. This is commonly used in applications that require high precision and stability.</p></p>
