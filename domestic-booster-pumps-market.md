<p><strong>The Domestic Booster Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Stage Pump</li><li>Multiple Stage Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The domestic booster pumps market consists of two main types: single stage pumps and multiple stage pumps. Single stage pumps have only one impeller, which provides a single stage of pressure boost. They are suitable for applications that require lower pressure boost, such as residential buildings. Conversely, multiple stage pumps have multiple impellers, which provide multiple stages of pressure boost, making them ideal for applications that require higher pressure boost, such as commercial buildings or high-rise apartments. These different types of pumps cater to varying pressure requirements in domestic water supply systems.</p></p>
