<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Roll grinders are machines used in the metal manufacturing industry to grind cylindrical rolls. Two common types of roll grinders are wheel-traverse type and table-traverse type. Wheel-traverse type roll grinders have a grinding wheel that moves across the workpiece, while the workpiece itself remains stationary. Table-traverse type roll grinders have a stationary grinding wheel, and the workpiece is moved across the grinding wheel. Both types offer different advantages depending on the specific requirements of the manufacturing process, such as the size and weight of the rolls being ground.</p></p>
