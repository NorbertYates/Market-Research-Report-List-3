<p><p>Textile printing inks are used to create designs and patterns on fabrics. Different types of textile printing inks cater to specific requirements. Reactive dye inks are commonly used for natural fibers and can provide vibrant and permanent colors. Acidic inks are suitable for protein fibers like wool and silk. Paint inks are used for hand painting on fabrics. Dispersion inks are water-based inks suitable for a wide range of fabrics. Sublimation inks are used for printing on synthetic fabrics through a heat transfer process.</p></p>
