<p><p>UPS Battery Packs are devices used to provide backup power during electrical outages or fluctuations. Internal Battery Packs are integrated within the UPS system and serve as a primary source of power. They are compact, non-removable, and typically provide short-term power backup. External Battery Packs are separate units that can be connected to the UPS system. They are usually larger and equipped with multiple batteries, allowing for extended power backup. They can be easily replaced or expanded, making them ideal for longer power outages or high-demand environments.</p></p>
