<p><p>Masking tape for floors is widely used in various industries and households for temporary floor protection during construction, renovation, maintenance, and painting. The market offers two types: single sided and double sided. Single sided masking tape is coated with adhesive on one side, and it is primarily used for creating straight lines and preventing paint bleed. Double sided masking tape, on the other hand, has adhesive on both sides, enabling it to securely hold down carpets, rugs, and other objects on the floor. Both options provide temporary adhesion and are easily removable without causing damage to the floor.</p></p>
