<p><strong>The Automatic Document Feeder (ADF) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Simplex (One Side Scanning)</li><li>Duplex (Two Side Scanning)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic Document Feeder (ADF) market types can be categorized into Simplex and Duplex. Simplex ADFs are capable of scanning documents on only one side, while Duplex ADFs have the ability to scan both sides of a document simultaneously. Simplex ADFs are suitable for basic document scanning needs, where scanning one side of the document is sufficient. On the other hand, Duplex ADFs are more advanced and provide faster scanning capabilities by scanning both sides of the document in a single pass.</p></p>
