<p><p>A pressure balanced expansion joint is a device that is used to absorb dimensional changes caused by thermal expansion in piping systems. It consists of a bellows element and a balancing bellows element enclosed within a housing. The bellows element absorbs the axial movement while the balancing bellows element counteracts the internal pressure force. This design enables the expansion joint to remain axially balanced and minimizes the forces exerted on the piping system, preventing damage and ensuring its longevity.</p><p>The pressure balanced expansion joint market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
