<p><p>Thermal Energy Storage (TES) systems are designed to store and release thermal energy to be used at a later time. These systems help in reducing energy consumption and greenhouse gas emissions by shifting the electrical load to off-peak hours when the electricity is cheaper and more abundant. TES systems are commonly used in residential, commercial, and industrial applications, including heating and cooling, power generation, and district heating.</p><p>The Thermal Energy Storage Systems Market is witnessing significant growth due to several factors. The market growth can be attributed to the increasing need for energy efficiency, rising focus on renewable energy, and the growing demand for sustainable solutions across various industries. Additionally, governments worldwide are implementing strict regulations to reduce carbon emissions, which is further boosting the adoption of TES systems.</p><p>Moreover, advancements in phase change materials (PCM) and thermal storage technologies are driving market growth. PCM materials such as salt hydrates, paraffin wax, and eutectic mixtures offer high energy storage capacity, low cost, and long-term stability, making them ideal for various TES applications.</p><p>In terms of latest trends, the integration of TES systems with solar power plants is gaining traction. This integration allows excess solar energy to be stored and utilized during non-sunny periods, thereby enhancing the overall efficiency and reliability of solar power generation. Furthermore, the adoption of TES systems in data centers is growing rapidly as data centers require consistent cooling, and TES systems offer efficient cooling capabilities.</p><p>Overall, the Thermal Energy Storage Systems Market is expected to experience steady growth in the forecast period, with a projected CAGR of 13.6%.
