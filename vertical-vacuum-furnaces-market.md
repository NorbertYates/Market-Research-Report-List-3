<p><p>Vertical vacuum furnaces are a type of industrial equipment used for various thermal processes, such as heat treating and brazing, in a vacuum environment. Single chamber vacuum furnaces consist of a single workspace for processing components. Double chamber vacuum furnaces have two separate workspaces, allowing for simultaneous processing and cooling. Multi chamber vacuum furnaces have multiple workspaces with independent controls, enabling different processes to be carried out simultaneously. These different types of vertical vacuum furnaces cater to the diverse needs and requirements of various industries.</p></p>
