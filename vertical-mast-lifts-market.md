<p><p>Vertical mast lifts can be categorized into two main types based on their power source: electric driven and engine driven. Electric driven vertical mast lifts are powered by electricity and are commonly used indoors or in environments where noise and emissions are a concern. Engine driven vertical mast lifts, on the other hand, are powered by gasoline or diesel engines and are often used in outdoor applications where access to power outlets may be limited.
