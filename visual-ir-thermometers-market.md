<p><p>Visual IR thermometers are devices that use infrared technology to measure temperature. There are two main market types for these thermometers: contact and non-contact. </p><p>Contact visual IR thermometers require direct physical contact with the object being measured. These devices use a temperature probe that comes into contact with the surface to provide accurate temperature readings.</p><p>On the other hand, non-contact visual IR thermometers can measure the temperature from a distance without making direct contact with the object. These devices use infrared sensors to detect the thermal radiation emitted by the object and provide temperature readings without any physical contact.</p><p>Both contact and non-contact visual IR thermometers have their advantages and applications, making them suitable for a range of industries including manufacturing, HVAC, food processing, and medical sectors.</p></p>
