<p><p>Automated Harmonic Generator is a device that is used to generate harmonic waveforms automatically. Harmonic waveforms are essentially distorted waveforms that are multiples of the fundamental frequency. These waves can cause issues in electrical power systems, leading to power quality problems and damaging sensitive equipment.</p><p>The market for Automated Harmonic Generators is expected to grow at a significant rate during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for power quality solutions in various industries, such as manufacturing, healthcare, and IT & telecommunications. As the complexity of electrical systems increases, the need for automated harmonic generators becomes crucial to maintain power quality and prevent damage to equipment.</p><p>The growing adoption of renewable energy sources, such as solar and wind power, also drives the demand for automated harmonic generators. These sources often produce harmonics due to the nature of their power generation process. Therefore, the installation of automated harmonic generators becomes necessary to minimize the impact of these harmonics on the power grid.</p><p>Furthermore, the increasing focus on energy efficiency and the need to comply with power quality standards and regulations also contribute to the market growth. Automated harmonic generators offer precise harmonic control and mitigation, ensuring compliance with power quality requirements.</p><p>In terms of trends, there is a growing shift towards the integration of advanced technologies in automated harmonic generators.
