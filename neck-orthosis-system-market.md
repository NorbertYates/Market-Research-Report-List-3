<p><ul><li>Inlay Type</li><li>Externally Assisted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Neck orthosis systems come in two main types: inlay type and externally assisted. Inlay type neck orthoses are worn directly on the body and provide support to the neck through a molded insert that conforms to the individual's neck shape. Externally assisted neck orthoses, on the other hand, consist of external structures such as straps or braces that are used to provide support and alignment to the neck. Both types of neck orthosis systems play a crucial role in managing neck pain and improving neck stability.</p></p>
