<p><p>Aqueous printing ink market types include flexography, gravure, and digital printing technologies. Flexography relies on flexible relief plates to transfer ink onto various substrates, while gravure uses engraved cylinders for high-quality printing on packaging materials. Digital printing technology offers rapid customization and short print runs without the need for plates. These diverse printing technologies cater to different industries and applications within the aqueous ink market, providing options for high-quality, efficient, and cost-effective printing solutions.</p></p>
