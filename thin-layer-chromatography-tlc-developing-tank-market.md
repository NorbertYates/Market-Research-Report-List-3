<p><p>A Thin Layer Chromatography (TLC) Developing Tank is a common laboratory instrument used for the separation and analysis of different compounds in a mixture. It consists of a chamber in which a solvent is allowed to travel up a thin layer of adsorbent, carrying the compounds with it as it moves. The compounds are separated based on their affinity to the adsorbent and their solubility in the solvent.</p><p>The Thin Layer Chromatography (TLC) Developing Tank Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.4% during the forecast period.
