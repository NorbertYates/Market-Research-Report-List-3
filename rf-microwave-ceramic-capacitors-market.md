<p><p>The RF & Microwave Ceramic Capacitors market includes two main types: Class 1 Ceramic Capacitors and Class 2 Ceramic Capacitors. Class 1 Ceramic Capacitors are known for their high stability and low losses, making them suitable for applications requiring precise capacitance values and stable behavior over a wide range of temperatures.
