<p><p>Automotive spray booths are widely used in the automotive industry for painting and finishing vehicles. There are different types of spray booths available in the market. </p><p>1. Air Pre-Filter: This type of filter is placed at the entrance of the spray booth and is designed to eliminate large particles from the incoming air, preventing them from entering the booth and contaminating the paint job.</p><p>2. Ceiling Filter: Positioned on the ceiling of the spray booth, the ceiling filter captures and removes smaller particles and contaminants from the air, ensuring a clean environment for painting.</p><p>3. Heat Exchanger: A heat exchanger helps in controlling the temperature inside the spray booth by transferring heat between the incoming and outgoing air. This helps to maintain optimal painting conditions and enhance the drying process.</p><p>4. Other market: This category includes various additional features and accessories available in the market, such as exhaust systems, ventilation systems, lighting options, control panels, and advanced filtration systems, offering a more comprehensive solution for automotive spray booths.</p></p>
